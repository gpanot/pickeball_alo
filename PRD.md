Phase 1 — 0 to 3 months

Club / Court management
1
F&B micro-POS wedge
Menu, player QR tap-to-pay, auto inventory, daily settlement. Replaces manual writing + photo verification loop.
2
Court schedule + slot blocking
Simple view: available / booked / maintenance. Staff can block courts without it showing as bookable.
3
Open play session creation
Manager creates a session: time, slots, level, entry fee. Auto-closes when full. No competition yet — just social play.
4
Staff check-in + no-show tracking
Mark who actually showed. Feeds trust score for open play participants over time.
5
Revenue dashboard
Daily view: court revenue + F&B + coaching commissions. Single number owner sees every morning.


Player
6
Credit wallet moat
Top up once (100k / 200k / 500k via VietQR). Spends on courts, coaches, drinks, open play. No re-entry of payment ever.
7
Court discovery with price filter
Filter by: sport, price range, distance, available now, indoor/outdoor. Kills Alobo's biggest UX problem immediately.
8
Court booking + cancellation
Book by hour, pay from wallet. Cancel with configurable policy (e.g. free up to 2hrs before). No QR screenshot loop.
9
Open play registration
Player sees: tonight's sessions near them, spots left, level, price. One tap to join, wallet charge, confirmed.
10
Booking history + receipts
Full log of all transactions. Downloadable. Useful for expats and corporate reimbursements.


Phase 2 — 3 to 6 months

Coach
11
Coach profile + public page
Photo, bio, specialties (beginner / competitive / drills), pricing, reviews. Shareable link for social media.
12
Availability calendar linked to court slots
Coach declares availability by court + time. Real-time sync — no double booking, no back-and-forth messages.
13
Book coach + court in one transaction
Player selects coach → sees available courts + times → one checkout. You book the court on their behalf, coach gets notified.
14
Lesson packages
Single session / 5-session bundle / monthly subscription. Bundle discount encourages commitment, improves coach LTV.
15
Coach earnings dashboard
Upcoming sessions, monthly income, pending payouts. Weekly disbursement to coach bank via VietQR.
16
Student progress log
Coach adds notes after each session. Player sees their history and can track improvement. Increases repeat bookings.
17
Substitute coach system
If coach cancels, system auto-offers available replacement from same court. Reduces churn from cancellations.


Club (additions)
18
Waitlist management
Court full → player joins waitlist → auto-notified when slot opens. Especially critical for peak hours.
19
Membership / session pass system
Sell 10-session packs or monthly unlimited at a discount. Locks in recurring revenue, reduces dependency on walk-ins.
20
Court maintenance scheduling
Block slots for resurfacing, cleaning, private events. Invisible to public booking, visible to staff.


Player (additions)
21
Notifications growth
Slot available, coach confirmed, open play starting in 1hr, price drop alert for nearby courts under your target.
22
Skill level + DUPR integration
Player declares level. System filters open play and coach recommendations to match. Reduces mismatch frustration.
23
Buddy system
Add friends, see when they are playing, join their session with one tap. Social hook that drives organic installs.
24
Player ratings
After open play, rate the session (court condition, game quality, reliability). Builds trust in listings over time.
25
Referral system growth
Invite a friend → both get 50k wallet credit. Cheapest acquisition channel in Vietnam, fuels wallet adoption.


Phase 3 — 6 to 12 months
26
Open play city feed
"Tonight near you: 3 open play sessions, 2 spots left at Friendly Pickleball." Cross-court discovery. Creates FOMO, fills empty courts.
27
White-label club app
Each court gets their own branded version. Their players, their QR, their colors. You run the backend. Premium tier pricing.
28
Wallet float + auto top-up
Player sets a minimum balance, auto top-up when below. You hold the float. Earn interest. Every low-balance moment is a touchpoint.
29
Tap-to-pay / NFC
Wristband or phone tap at F&B counter debits wallet directly. Zero friction for repeat drink purchases mid-session.
30
Light competition layer
Weekly ladder, small prize pool funded by entry fees. Not full tournament brackets yet — just enough to create stakes and keep players coming back.
